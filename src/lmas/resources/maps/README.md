# Bundled LMAS map vectors

These compact NumPy vector files were generated from `basemap-data` 2.0.0 boundary datasets and simplified for interactive LMAS underlays. They provide offline coastlines, national boundaries, state/province lines, and United States county boundaries.

The GSHHG-derived coastline and political-boundary source data are distributed under LGPL-3.0-or-later. Other `basemap-data` assets, including the United States county source used by LMAS, carry the package's MIT notice. LMAS preserves the applicable LGPL, GPL, and MIT texts in the top-level `licenses/` directory. The bundled vector derivatives remain under those upstream terms.
